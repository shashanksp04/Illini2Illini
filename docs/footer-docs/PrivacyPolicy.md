# 🔒 Privacy Policy

## Privacy Policy for Illini2Illini

**Effective Date:** May 2026

---

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Illini2Illini collects, uses, and protects your information.

By using the Platform, you consent to this Policy.

---

## 2. Information Collected

### 2.1 Information You Provide

* First and last name
* Email address (`@illinois.edu`)
* Username
* Profile picture
* Listing content and associated data

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

* Usage data (e.g., page interactions, feature usage)
* Device and browser metadata

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Use

We use your data to:

* Operate and maintain the Platform
* Display listings and user profiles
* Enable user-to-user contact
* Enforce Terms and prevent abuse
* Improve functionality and performance

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not sell or rent personal data**.

We may share data:

### 4.1 With Other Users

* Profile and listing information
* Contact email only after explicit user action

### 4.2 With Service Providers

Third-party services (e.g., hosting, database, authentication) process data solely to operate the Platform.

### 4.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

---

## 5. Data Visibility

### Public Users:

* Limited listing data
* Username only

### Verified Users:

* Full listing details
* Name and profile picture
* Contact email (only after reveal action)

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data:

* As long as necessary to operate the Platform
* As required for legal, security, or operational purposes

We may retain certain data after account deletion where necessary.

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures.

However:

* No system is completely secure
* You assume risk when transmitting data online

---

## 8. User Control

You may:

* Edit your profile and listings
* Request account deletion via email

We are not obligated to delete data immediately where retention is necessary.

We are not responsible for their practices.

## 10. No Guarantee of Anonymity

Information you share may be visible to other users.

You are responsible for what you choose to disclose.
