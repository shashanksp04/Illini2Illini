### Our Story

Illini2Illini started with a simple frustration.

At UIUC, finding housing is harder than it should be.

Students searching for apartments, roommates, or subleases are forced to jump between scattered platforms—Reddit threads, Facebook groups, leasing portals, spreadsheets, and university boards. There’s no single place where everything lives, and even when you do find something, it’s often incomplete, outdated, or hard to trust.

At the same time, demand for housing has only increased.

With rising enrollment and policies like the new **University Housing lottery system**, more students are pushed into uncertain housing situations. Instead of a predictable process, many now rely on last-minute options, temporary arrangements, or fragmented listings across the internet.

The result is a system that is:

* Hard to navigate
* Time-consuming
* Unstructured
* Often unreliable

Illini2Illini was built to fix that.

---

### What We’re Building

Illini2Illini is a **UIUC-only housing marketplace** designed to make it easier for students to find and share housing opportunities.

Instead of unstructured posts and scattered information, the platform provides:

* **Structured listings** with all essential details
* **Powerful filtering** to quickly find relevant options
* **Verified UIUC users** to improve trust and safety
* **A centralized place** for the entire student community

It’s not a leasing platform, and it doesn’t handle payments or contracts.

It’s simply a better way for students to **find each other and connect**.

---

### Why Verification Matters

One of the biggest problems with existing platforms is trust.

Anyone can post on Reddit or Facebook. It’s often unclear:

* Who the person is
* Whether the listing is real
* Whether the information is accurate

Illini2Illini solves this by requiring:

* A verified `@illinois.edu` email
* A completed profile
* Identity tied to a real UIUC student

This creates a safer, more reliable environment where students can interact with confidence.

---

### Built by Students, for Students

Illini2Illini is built by a fellow UIUC student who experienced these problems firsthand.

The goal isn’t to build a company-first product.

It’s to build something that:

* Actually helps students
* Reduces stress during housing searches
* Brings structure to a chaotic system

No monetization. No hidden incentives.

Just a tool designed to make housing at UIUC easier.

---

### Looking Ahead

The focus is simple:

Make it easier for students to find housing—quickly, clearly, and safely.

If Illini2Illini can save even a few students from the stress of endlessly scrolling through scattered posts, negotiating blindly, or missing good opportunities, then it’s doing its job.
