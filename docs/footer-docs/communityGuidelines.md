# 🛡️ Community Guidelines

## Illini2Illini Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** May 2026

---

## 1. Purpose

Illini2Illini is built to help UIUC students find housing in a **structured, safe, and trustworthy environment**.

These guidelines define what is allowed on the platform and what is not.

By using Illini2Illini, you agree to follow these rules.

---

## 2. Core Principles

All users are expected to:

* Be **honest**
* Be **respectful**
* Be **clear and accurate**
* Act in **good faith**

If you’re unsure whether something is appropriate, it probably isn’t.

---

## 3. Listing Guidelines

### You MUST:

* Post only housing you are **authorized to share**
* Provide **accurate and complete information**
* Use **real photos** of the unit (no misleading images)
* Clearly state:

  * Rent
  * Availability
  * Room type
* Keep descriptions **truthful and not exaggerated**

---

### You MUST NOT:

* Post fake or non-existent listings
* Misrepresent price, availability, or conditions
* Use stock photos or unrelated images
* Post duplicate or spam listings
* List properties you do not have rights to sublease or advertise

---

## 4. Communication Guidelines

When contacting or being contacted:

### You MUST:

* Communicate respectfully
* Be clear and direct
* Respect boundaries

---

### You MUST NOT:

* Harass, threaten, or pressure other users
* Send spam or repeated unwanted messages
* Mislead users during conversations
* Ask for sensitive personal or financial information unnecessarily

---

## 5. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited:

* Scams or fraudulent activity
* Impersonation of another person
* Hate speech or discriminatory behavior
* Illegal activity of any kind
* Posting malicious links or harmful content
* Attempting to exploit or bypass platform rules

---

## 6. Safety Guidelines (Strongly Recommended)

Illini2Illini does not mediate transactions. You are responsible for your own safety.

We strongly recommend:

* Never send money without verifying the listing
* Meet in person or request a video tour
* Verify lease terms directly with landlords when possible
* Do not share sensitive personal information
* Trust your instincts — if something feels off, walk away

---

## 7. Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report listings that violate these guidelines.

### What happens when a listing is reported:

* Listings may be **reviewed and removed immediately**
* Users may be **warned, restricted, or banned**
* Listings with multiple reports may be **automatically removed**

Enforcement is:

* At our sole discretion
* Not subject to appeal or explanation

---

## 8. Account Consequences

Violations may result in:

* Listing removal
* Temporary suspension
* Permanent account ban

Serious violations may lead to **immediate termination without warning**.

---

## 9. No Platform Responsibility

Illini2Illini does not:

* Verify listings
* Guarantee user behavior
* Mediate disputes

You are responsible for your interactions and decisions.

---

## 10. Changes to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated at any time.

Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance.
